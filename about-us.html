Our Company

Himalaya Bottling Company was incorporated in the year 2001. We are manufacturers of Sagar Carbonated Soft Drinks(Sagar Orange, Sagar Lemon, Sagar Cola, Sagar Club Soda and Sagar Mango)which are available in various sizes. Our company is led by diligent management and are committed to provide high quality beverages to our customers. We value relatonship with our distributors and believe that working and growing together is a key to lead the market. We presently distribute our products across Rajasthan and Madhya Pradesh and are set to penetrate other states soon through our dedicated distribution network.

Our Mission

To provide excellent quality beverages to our customers at most affordable prices. To cater to the tastes and likes of all the generations. To grow as a beloved brand of our customers.

Quality Standards

We are commited to produce high quality beverages, our products meet the quality standards set by the health ministry for carbonated drinks and are in compliance set by FSSAI (22212034000846). We use RO Procedures for water processing insuring high quality water . We have regular checks of all ingredients to ensure good quality. We maintain regular feedback system from customers to enhance our quality procedures.

Infrastructure & Manufacturing Facilities

We are equipped with advanced and fully automatic plants such as Automatic RO plants, Automatic Chilling plants, Automatic Washing, filling and packaging plants along with a extremely qualified and experienced team to supervise the manufacturing and production of best quality beverages.

Our Future Plans

We are set to expand distribution of our products across UP and Gujarat in the immediate future.
