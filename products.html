Sagar Orange

Sagar Orange Drink is manufactured using top grade materials and intermediates. Its available at market leading prices and in different packaging options.

Usage:

Sagar orange is used as refreshing & energetic drink.

Sagar orange drink ingredients:

Carbonated water (6-Stage treated ), Sugar, Permitted class–I, Carbonated Soft Drink Preservatives, Citric acid, Permitted synthetic color & and added Orange flavors ( Nature identical & artificial flavoring substances). contains no fruit.

Microbiological Status:

Sagar Orange is free from any pathogenic bacteria. The product is safe to human health from the microbiological point of view.

Preparation:

The preparation process includes batch preparation, carbonation, proportioning, filling & packing.

Packing:

Sagar Orange is packed in 250ml, 500ml, 1Ltr & 2Ltrs pack sizes. Pet bottle duly labeled and further packed in corrugated boxes.

Storage Recommendations:

The finished product can be stored at ambient temperature, under normal condition. Higher temperature & sunlight should be avoided to enhance shelf life.
